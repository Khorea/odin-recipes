Pumpkin Spice Latte Recipe

This homemade pumpkin spice latte recipe is quick and easy to make, more affordable than the coffee shop version, and lightly sweetened with maple syrup. Feel free to top with whipped cream if you’d like!

Ingredients:

  • Espresso or strong coffee
  • Milk
  • Pumpkin purée
  • Pumpkin pie spice
  • Maple syrup
  • Vanilla extract and fine sea salt

Steps:

  1. Make a big batch of the syrup to have on hand
  2. Adjust the sweetener to taste
  3. Froth the milk however you prefer (or skip that step)
